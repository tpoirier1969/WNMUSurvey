About this survey

This five-stage questionnaire asks about who WNMU-TV reaches, how people watch, what they value, and where the station should improve. Most questions are optional, and some questions change based on earlier answers.

Privacy in this test version

Drafts and submitted test responses are stored only in this browser. A random respondent ID supports linked questionnaires without putting a name or email address in answer records. If you separately request contact after submitting, that name and email are stored in a separate local contact record and research results show only an aggregate request count. Public production will require approved protected storage and a final privacy notice.
