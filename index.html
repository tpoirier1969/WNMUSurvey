What this is designed to learn

01

Where viewers are

Broadcast, cable, satellite, apps, livestreams, online video, and the devices and times people actually use.

02

What they expect

Which station roles matter most, then how viewers believe WNMU-TV performs in those same areas.

03

What they would watch

Programming interest, local formats, top-five priorities, and open suggestions for missing subjects.

04

Who those interests represent

Optional location, age, household, internet, education, and employment information for audience comparison.
