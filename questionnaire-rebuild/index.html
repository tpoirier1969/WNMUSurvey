About this survey

This five-stage questionnaire asks about who WNMU-TV reaches, how people watch, what they value, and where the station should improve. After submitting, respondents may choose from optional linked follow-up questionnaires.

Privacy

Your answers, drafts, private follow-up access record, and optional follow-up responses are stored in this browser during testing. Main and follow-up responses are linked with random pseudonymous IDs. The questionnaire does not ask for your name or email address.
